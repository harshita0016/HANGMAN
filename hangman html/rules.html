🎮 Welcome to Hang-The-Man

📜 Game Rules

🔹 Objective:

Guess the hidden word one letter at a time before you run out of attempts. Each wrong guess brings you closer to being “Hanged” in the game.

🔹 Rules of the Game:

  • You start with 5 wrong attempts.
  • Enter one letter at a time to guess the word.
  • If the letter is correct, it will be revealed in the word.
  • If the letter is wrong, one attempt is lost and the hangman drawing progresses.
  • You win if you guess the word before attempts run out.
  • You lose if the hangman drawing is completed.

🔹 Example:

Word Guess Result
_ _ _ _ _ E Correct → _ E _ _ _
_ E _ _ _ X Wrong → Attempts left: 4

Keep guessing wisely before it’s too late!

✨ Tip: Think smart, guess vowels first (a, e, i, o, u)! ✨
